How the levy is calculated
The levy is charged as a percentage of the total construction value (excluding GST) when the value equals or exceeds the threshold. Example: $500,000 construction value × 0.575% = $2,875.00 levy.

What happens next

1
Council will review your application within 10 business days to confirm it's a Properly Made Application (PMA). If anything is missing, you'll receive an Action Notice.
2
Your application enters the assessment period. The assessing officer will contact you if an Information Request is required.
3
You can track your application status at ePlanning using your DA reference number above.
